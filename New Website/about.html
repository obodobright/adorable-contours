Brand Story

Adorable Contours, a prominent fashion hub based in Lagos, Nigeria, specializes in Ankara, the vibrant and iconic African print fabric. Established in 2010 by Ijy Hilary, an avid fashion enthusiast, the boutique quickly became renowned for its exquisite Ankara collections that celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Nigeria and the African continent.

From its inception, Adorable Contours has been dedicated to showcasing the versatility and beauty of Ankara through its carefully curated designs. Ijy's passion for the fabric and her commitment to promoting Nigerian craftsmanship have made Adorable Contours a go-to destination for Ankara enthusiasts. The boutique offers a wide range of Ankara clothing, including traditional attire, modern dresses, skirts, trousers,tops, and accessories, all crafted with meticulous attention to detail and quality. Each piece reflects the fusion of traditional aesthetics with contemporary styles, making them suitable for various occasions, from casual outings to formal events.

Adorable Contours also collaborates with local artisans and designers to bring unique and exclusive Ankara pieces to its customers, further supporting the growth of Nigeria's fashion industry. With its focus on Ankara and its dedication to preserving and promoting African culture through fashion, Adorable Contours has become a beloved institution in Lagos and beyond, empowering individuals to express their identity and heritage through clothing that is as beautiful as it is meaningful. Thank you for choosing Adorable Contours. We look forward to being part of your fashion journey!
